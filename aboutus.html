Our Story

Welcome to the Village Hidden in the Leaves! The First Hokage founded this village with the dream of providing a place where otaku can converge and enjoy great food in an anime inspired atmosphere.Anime is a huge part of our lives and we know how much it means to people. We want to bring that world into real life to for people to enjoy.

Covid Guidelines

  1. No Reservations: We are a walk in only/first come first serve establishment. Please EXPECT LONGER wait times due to CDC guidelines and limited seating.
  2. Too Hot for Ramen: Upon arrival, each person will be required to have a mask on throughout their time at Naroodle unless eating at a table. There will be a temperature screening at the door. If anyone in your party has a fever, the entire party will be turned away.
  3. To be Fair to all Ninja: There will be a 45 min-1 hour time limit that your party has at the table. This is so we can accommodate as many guests as possible throughout the day.
